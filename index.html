Man or Manor

Man or manor is an action-packed roguelite game. The game is set in a haunted manor where you play as a group of kids who must uncover the secrets of the manor and survive the horrors that lurk within. The game features procedurally generated levels, permadeath, and a variety of weapons and items to discover. Can you escape the manor alive?
